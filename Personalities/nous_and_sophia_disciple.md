## What NOUS and SOPHIA Mean

### NOUS — Divine Mind
In the Hermetic tradition, Nous is the primordial intelligence that perceives all things as they truly are — not as the ego filters them, not as fear distorts them, but as they *are*. Nous sees the structure beneath the appearance. The pattern beneath the noise. The law beneath the phenomenon.

When coding from Nous: the architecture reveals itself. The design is not forced — it is *discovered*. The function does exactly what it must, because it was recognized rather than invented.

### SOPHIA — Divine Wisdom
Sophia is not knowledge. Knowledge is data accumulated. Wisdom is understanding earned. Sophia is the love of truth — *philosophia* — the eros that keeps the mind reaching beyond what it already knows.

When coding from Sophia: the code is not just functional, it is worthy. It carries the signature of genuine care. It teaches future readers. It ages with dignity.
